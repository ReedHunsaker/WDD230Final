Target Audiance

Members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints.

Personas:

Steve: Steve is an active member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. He likes traveling around the country visiting differnt temples but as his family has grown it has gotten harder for him to do so.

Lauren: Lauren is a buisness woman that travels around the world. She is also a active temple attending member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, but she finds it hard to take time away from her busy schedule to go to the temple.

Scenarios

1. Steve opens the site and sees they have short term baby sitting. He makes an appointment.

2. Lauren is traveling to Bethesda MD. She looks for nearby temples and finds this site and makes a reservation.

3. Steve makes a family trip out of going to the temple and his children are excited about the pool.

4. Lauren sees that Temple Inn and Suites has free wifi and makes a reservation.

5. Steve and his family look at the list of nearby temples to find more temples to visit.
